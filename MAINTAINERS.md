## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome contributions from the community! Active contributors who demonstrate:
- Consistent high-quality contributions
- Good understanding of the project architecture
- Collaborative approach to development
- Commitment to code review and community support

May be invited to become maintainers.

### Path to Maintainership

1. **Contributor** - Make quality contributions (code, docs, reviews)
2. **Active Contributor** - Regular contributions over 3+ months
3. **Trusted Contributor** - Demonstrate deep project knowledge
4. **Maintainer Nomination** - Proposed by existing maintainer
5. **Maintainer** - Approved by project lead
